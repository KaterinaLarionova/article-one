Vancouver

This outdoorsy city is made for long, scenic runs. Stay at a waterfront property (like The Westin Bayshore) and you’ll be perfectly situated near the famed Seawall, which is the world’s longest uninterrupted waterfront path stretching nearly 14 miles. With plenty of gorgeous views of the mountains, waterfront, and forests within the thousand-acre Stanley Park, your miles will fly.
